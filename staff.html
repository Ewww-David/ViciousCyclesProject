John Doe1 joined VisiousCycles in April 2019 and became GM in Oct 2019. He comes to ViciousCycles with over 36 years in the Powersports Industry! He got into the industry after being an amateur roadracer on his 1982 Kawasaki GPz550 at Sears Point Raceway and then moving to Southern California joining the Powersports industry officially. Husband and father of two fantastic women and aspiring author, John Doe1 has managed several multi-line metric stores covering all the Japanese and European brands and was the factory Honda HRC (Honda Race Corp) Liaison for North America. After moving to NC in 2009 he took the helm as the GSM for Ray Price Harley Davidson Triumph from 2009 - 2017. Following that tenure he became the National Brand Manager for Stealth Electric Bikes out of Australia traveling all around the country and Australia. John Doe1 say's he is thrilled to be back with Honda and Yamaha here with ViciousCycles close to home and those brands he started his career in! Long time rider who's enthusiasm has never waivered. John Doe1 has been involved with several Top 10 & Top 50 Dealers in America. His extensive knowledge in all brands and the industry along with franchise addition will help ViciousCycles going forward with our big expansion plans and increased new and pre-owned sales. service, and parts.
